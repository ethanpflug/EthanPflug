Gentle Hatha Yoga
Intended for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded
foundation in the practice of yoga, this 60 minute class of poses
and slow movement focuses on asana (proper alignment and
posture), pranayama (breath work), and guided meditation to
foster your mind and body connection.

Restorative Yoga
This 90 minute class features very slow movement and long poses
that are supported by a chair or wall. This calming, restorative
experience is suitable for students of any level of experience.
This practice can be a perfect way to help rehabilitate an injury.
